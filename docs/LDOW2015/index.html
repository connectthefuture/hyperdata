Introduction

There have been major changes in the Web environment in recent years. For the Web developer these have probably been most significant in two areas: the massive increase in the use of mobile devices and the improvements in browsers that enable more complex software to run client-side in the browser. In the near future it seems probable that there will also be a growth in the network-enabling of other consumer products (cars, cookers, ) in the "Internet of Things".

The Linked Data community is well-advised and well-positioned to embrace these changes. In general, to exploit innovations it is best to have frameworks (development methodologies, system architectures and so on) in place that are flexible and well-suited to rapid change and
